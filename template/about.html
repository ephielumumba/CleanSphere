CleanSphere is a non profit organisation that educate citizen scientist to collect data on water, soil and air pollution, contributing to policy advocacy.

What we do at CleanSphere

At CleanSphere, we are dedicated to transforming the way communities understand and address pollution. By harnessing advanced technologies like IoT sensors, satellite imagery, and AI-driven analytics, we track pollution in real time, providing precise and actionable data on key environmental indicators such as air quality, water contamination, and waste management.

But we don't stop at data collection. At CleanSphere, we believe that informed communities are empowered communities. Through our user-friendly platform, we provide individuals, organizations, and policymakers with the insights they need to take meaningful action. Our mission extends to fostering grassroots movements by equipping citizen scientists with the tools to monitor their local environments, raise awareness, and advocate for sustainable solutions.

Our efforts have already sparked measurable progress—from identifying pollution hotspots that led to cleaner rivers, to influencing policies aimed at reducing industrial emissions. By bridging the gap between cutting-edge technology and community-driven advocacy, CleanSphere is not just tracking pollution—we’re inspiring change for a healthier, more sustainable future.
